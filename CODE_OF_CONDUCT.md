# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, direct, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- assume good intent
- focus on the code and product
- give actionable feedback
- keep collaboration professional

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- deliberate disruption
- publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or reject contributions that do not meet this standard.
